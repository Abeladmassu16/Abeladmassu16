Onboard Dining

We offer a wide range of meals, from authentic Chinese to world favorite cuisine; our meals are carefully cooked with fresh ingredients to meet your palate.

Premium Business/Business & Premium Economy Passengers Main Course Pre-order Service

Out-bound flights from Taiwan(excluding RMQ): Main course pre-order service is available. You are welcome to choose the cuisine co-branded with THE MICHELIN Green Star restaurant Yang Ming Spring. In-bound flights to Taiwan on all long-haul routes and on some regional routes (currently open to HND/NRT/FUK/NGO/CTS/KIX/ICN/CGK/DPS/KUL/PEN/ BKK/SGN/SIN/HAN), the main course pre-order service is available. After confirm your booking, business class meal selections can be made earlier than 21 days before or no later than 24 hours prior to your scheduled flight via our E-menu on the website.

Notes:
  • For unforeseen reasons, the meals served during your flight may not match those shown here and may temporarily suspend the Premium Business/Business Class passengers main course pre-order service.
  • If your flight schedule, flight sector or cabin class is changed, your meal order will be cancelled automatically. Please place a new meal order at your convenience. Waitlisted is unable to make meal selections.
  • Meal selection service is not available for some Extra flights, charter flights, unscheduled and code-sharing flights. Due to system and operation reasons, business class pre-order meal service is not applicable on the table above.
