Who we work with

Governments and leaders

Every part of FERESE work involves strong collaboration with government leaders. Political will and leadership are essential agents of change to improve the health and well-being of everyone, everywhere.

Experts

FERESE brings together individual experts to provide scientific, technical and strategic advice on specific health issues. These experts act in a personal capacity and do not represent any external entity, authority or government.
